Verbal Inflection

My Doctoral work

My work investigates Spanish verbs in Distributed Morphology, a syntax-centric approach to the syntax-morphology interface. The overall approach taken in the dissertation is twofold. While other scholars who have treated verbal inflection as primarily a linear arrangement of morphemes, my dissertation advocates having an adequate semantic analysis of temporal and modal categories to avoid artificially divorcing the morphosyntax of verbs from their morphosemantics. The second approach calls for a reduction to the amount of conceptual machinery needed to analyze Spanish verbs.
